PAWNDER: RULES OF PLAY

OBJECTIVE:
The first player to reach 10 Captures wins the game.

ON YOUR TURN:
Step 1. Make one standard pawn Chess move (optional).
Step 2. Either:
- Draw a card and play it
- Draw a card and place it in a reserve spot
- Play a reserve card

MOVEMENT:
- Pawns can only move 1 space forward.
- Pawns can attack 1 space diagonally, but ONLY if the target is in the Field of Play (Rows 3-6).
- You cannot attack pieces sitting in the Safe Zones.

CARDS:
- If your Reserves are full, you MUST play a drawn card. 
- Block cards are stashed automatically and trigger a 10-second interrupt if your opponent tries to capture your pawn.

RESCUE:
If you move a pawn into the opponent's starting Safe Zone, you may select any open square in your own Safe Zone to bring a captured pawn back into play!
